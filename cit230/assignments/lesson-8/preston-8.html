Fun Facts: Did you Know?

Many do not know about the small town of Preston, Idaho. It is relatively quiet and a small farming community. With the small quiet area of Preston, Idaho, what it is known for is this is the town where they filmed the movie "Napoleon Dynamite". You can see many of the actual buildings and the school where these shots were taken. We encourage you to go take a look!
